Anticyberpunk - a genre of science fiction that, while set in an oppressive society, does not
always take place purely in a lawless subculture but rather in normal every day life, while still
dominated by computer technology. It maintains the bleak aesthetic of cyberpunk.

what led up to the civil unrest, to the aftermath eighty years
in the future when America speaks a new dialect that
